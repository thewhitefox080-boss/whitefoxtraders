About White Fox Traders
Authorized reseller of Anker, Soundcore & UGreen in Nepal

Who we are

White Fox Traders is a registered sole proprietorship business based in Bhaktapur, Nepal. We are an authorized reseller of three of the world's leading electronics brands — Anker, Soundcore, and UGreen — bringing genuine, warranty-backed products to customers across Nepal.

Our promise

Every product we sell is 100% genuine and comes with the official manufacturer warranty. We do not sell replicas or grey-market products. What you see is what you get.

Payment & delivery

We accept eSewa, FonePay, and Cash on Delivery. We deliver across Nepal within 2–3 business days. Free delivery on all orders.

Delivery Info
Fast delivery across Nepal

Delivery time

Orders are delivered within 2–3 business days across Nepal. Kathmandu Valley orders may arrive sooner. Orders placed before 12 PM are processed the same day.

Delivery charges

Delivery is free on all orders. No minimum order value required.

Coverage

We deliver to all districts across Nepal including Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, Lalitpur, Pokhara, Chitwan, Butwal, Birgunj, Biratnagar, and more.

Order tracking

Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation via email (if provided). Our team will contact you on your phone number before delivery.

Warranty Policy
Official manufacturer warranty on all products

Anker & Soundcore

All Anker and Soundcore products come with an 18-month official warranty from the date of purchase.

UGreen

All UGreen products come with a 24-month (2-year) official warranty from the date of purchase.

What is covered

Warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. It does not cover physical damage, water damage, or misuse.

How to claim

To claim warranty, contact us at thewhitefox080@gmail.com with your order details and a description of the issue. We will guide you through the process.

Returns Policy
Please read carefully before purchasing

No returns policy

All sales are final. We do not accept returns or exchanges once an order has been delivered. Please review your order carefully before confirming.

Damaged or wrong item

If you receive a damaged product or the wrong item, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos. We will resolve it promptly.

Warranty claims

Product defects after delivery are covered under the manufacturer warranty. Please refer to our Warranty Policy for details.
