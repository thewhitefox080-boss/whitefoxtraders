# White Fox Traders

Official e-commerce website for **White Fox Traders** — authorized reseller of Anker, Soundcore & UGreen electronics in Nepal.

## 🛍️ Features
- 82 products across 3 brands (Anker, Soundcore, UGreen)
- Filter by brand, category and price range
- Search products
- Shopping cart
- Checkout with eSewa & FonePay payment options
- Fully responsive design

## 🏪 About
White Fox Traders is a registered electronics business based in Lakhe Tol-3, Changunarayan, Bhaktapur, Nepal. We are an authorized reseller of:
- **Anker** — Cables, chargers, power banks, accessories
- **Soundcore** — Earbuds, headphones, Bluetooth speakers
- **UGreen** — Earbuds, chargers, power banks, cables

## 💳 Payment Methods

- FonePay
